{% trans "Children's geographies an initiative aimed at rethinking the city from child’s perspective, created in 2015 and based mostly in Kyiv, Ukraine. Since then, we are aimed to establish a feeling of presence and involvement of children in the city processes through art, research, and educational practices." %}

{% trans "What we do:" %}

  • {% trans "share suссessful practices of child-friendly spaces with a broad audience" %}
  • {% trans "build an understanding and basic knowledge of city planning and architecture for children" %}
  • {% trans "engage a young audience to the processes of discussion and transformation of the city" %}
  • {% trans "through arts and play, encourage children to understand their own possibilities to influence and change the city" %}
  • {% trans "draw attention to rights and needs of children in society and the city." %}
